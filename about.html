Our Philosophy

Large AI service providers are consolidating power and dictating the rules of how we use modern AI tools: who gets access to which models, under what terms, and for what price.

At AI Rigs, we believe that your AI models, your data, and your workflows should stay in your control, while running on hardware that you own. The benefits of running local-first AI are numerous:

  • Privacy First: Have full control over your sensitive data.
  • Low Latency: Local inference without the cloud overhead.
  • Fixed Cost: Predictable ROI without changing pricing terms.
  • Flexible Customization: AI systems built for your specific needs.

Our mission

We want to provide local-first AI solutions for individuals and small to medium-sized businesses, while paying special attention to user experience and offering an excellent support service.
